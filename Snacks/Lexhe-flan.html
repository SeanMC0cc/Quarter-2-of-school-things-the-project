Leche flan For 6 people

Leche Flan is a dessert made with eggs and milk creating a sweet taste. This dish is a regular dish found in Filipino restaurants.

-10 pieces eggs
-1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
-1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
-1 cup granulated sugar
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract
