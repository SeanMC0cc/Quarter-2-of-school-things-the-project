Macaroni

Macaroni salad is just a type of salad with macaroni but with a twist it is cold with fruits, fish, or meat

-1 lb. elbow macaroni
-1 1/2 cup Lady's Choice Mayonnaise
-12 oz. canned tuna flakes drained
-1 medium red onion minced
-1 cup minced celery heart
-6 tablespoons cup sweet pickle relish
-1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 teaspoons salt
-1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
-10 cups water
